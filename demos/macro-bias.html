Macro Bias

Macro Bias is a daily regime research product: it turns a macro snapshot into a next-session score with a risk size, not a price target. This demo mirrors the product idea: find the K nearest historical sessions in feature space (cosine distance), inverse-distance weight their forward returns, and map the blend through a gate (including sit-out when fear is extreme and edge is tiny). This page is an interactive sketch of the scoring system for demonstration purposes.
