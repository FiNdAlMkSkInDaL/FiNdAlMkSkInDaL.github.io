Why the gate exists

Every capture path hits one deterministic gate before persistence: size limits, secret/credential/payment rejects, then at-rest protection and promote into local memory. Agents never see raw captures that fail the gate — matching the real identityd pipeline.

What is this? Identity keeps user context in a local encrypted .me vault. Agents only receive a small ranked slice of facts for a task, then drop session state. Captures are filtered by a safety gate before they can enter memory.
