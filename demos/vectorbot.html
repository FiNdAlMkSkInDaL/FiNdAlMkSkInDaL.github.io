Probe output - predicted probability distribution
The linear probe turns the 768-dimensional activation vector into a distribution over the 7 possible actions (sums to ~100%).
These are the model's "beliefs" right after the forward pass. The gate then decides whether to act or ABSTAIN.

Natural language is mapped to a small set of bot actions via a frozen model activation and a probe — not by generating free-form text. Safe or unknown commands are rejected (ABSTAIN); accepted ones update the grid. This page is an interactive sketch of zero-generation latent routing for demonstration purposes.
